Dal Tadka

Sizzling hot Dal Dadka!(yellow lentil curry)

Healthy and tasty recipe famous in North India. Usually served with Indian flat bread (roti).

Ingredients

  • Yellow Lentil (Dal)
  • Water
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Salt
  • Red Chilly Powder
  • Turmeric
  • Coriander Powder
  • Olive Oil
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Red Chilly Whole
  • Asafoetida (Hing)
  • Coriander Leaves

Step-by-Step

  1. Firstly, soak 1 cup of dal in warm water for atleast 30min before cooking.
  2. Meanwhile, chop up the tomatoes and onions.
  3. Now, strain the dal and add into a pressure cooker.
  4. Add 1:3 ratio water (so 3 cups) to the cooker.
  5. Boil the dal.
  6. Now, add in the spices in low quantity into the cooker. (We'll add more at the end!)
  7. Now, close the lid and cook till 3 whistles.
  8. After that, prepare the first tempering: Add some oil to a small pan.
  9. Now, add cumin seeds, dry spices and salt and pepper to it along with tomatoes and onions.
  10. Cook for 5-10min and then pour the contents into the cooker.
  11. Mix well.
  12. Now for the second tempering, again heat a small pan and add 1-2 tbsp of oil.
  13. Then, add 1/2 tsp of asafoetida to it.
  14. Add 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste.
  15. Add 2-3 whole red chillies.
  16. Now, add the contents to the dal. (sizzling!!!)
  17. Serve hot with roti :)
