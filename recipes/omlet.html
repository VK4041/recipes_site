Omlet

Delicately cooked eggs with choice of veggies and cheese!

2-3 eggs per serving, 20 grams of protein and 400 calories

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Butter/Oil
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Green Chillies
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cheese Slice
  • Bread Slice

Step-by-Step

  1. Chop all veggies finely beforehand.
  2. Crack open 2 to 3 eggs into a bowl.
  3. Add some salt and pepper to it.
  4. Whisk the eggs/beat with a fork till fluffy.
  5. Add the chopped veggies.
  6. Heat up a pan and add 1 tbsp of butter/oil to it.
  7. Now, add the omlet batter to the pan.
  8. Cook on low heat for 3-4 min.
  9. Flip the omlet and add a cheese slice or two if you like :)
  10. FInally, serve with a piece of toasted bread.
