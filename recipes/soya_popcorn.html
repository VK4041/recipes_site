Soya Popcorn

Air fried soya chunk popcorns!

Easy and healthy protein rich snack made in air-fryer/oven

Ingredients

  • 50g soya chunks
  • Water
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Yogurt
  • Corn Flour
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Red Chilly Powder
  • Turmeric
  • Coriander Powder
  • Olive Oil

Step-by-Step

  1. Take 50g of raw soya chunks and boil them in water for 5min to make them soft.
  2. Then, add the cooked soya chunks to ice-cold water or running cold water to stop cooking process.
  3. Now, add these to a bowl.
  4. Add in the spices: salt, pepper, red chilly powder, turmeric and coriander powder.
  5. Add 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste.
  6. Add 1-2 tbsp yogurt.
  7. Add 2 tbsp cornflour.
  8. Mix well and then add some olive oil to make them crispy.
  9. Now, to your air fryer, add the contents of the bowl gently onto the tray.
  10. Then, cook at 180 degree C for 10min, then stir and cook till crisp.
  11. Finally, serve hot and crispy soya popcorn with your favourite dip!
